Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Data We Collect

We collect the information you voluntarily provide when creating an account and adding expenses — such as transaction amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions. We also collect basic account information like your email address.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide and improve the Spendly service. This includes generating spending summaries, category breakdowns, and date-based filtering. We do not use your personal financial data for advertising or any other commercial purposes.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly may use third party services for hosting, analytics, or payment processing. These services are required to operate the app and are obligated to handle your data in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, or if you would like to request deletion of your account and data, please contact us at privacy@spendly.app.
